Achievements

Individual Awards

  • Ballon d'Or: Won 8 times (2009, 2010, 2011, 2012, 2015, 2019, 2021, 2023).
  • FIFA Best Player Award: Won in 2019.
  • Copa América Best Player: Won in 2021.
  • FIFA U-20 World Cup Golden Ball: Won in 2005.

With Clubs

  • La Liga: Won 10 times with Barcelona.
  • UEFA Champions League: Won 4 times with Barcelona (2006, 2009, 2011, 2015).
  • Copa del Rey: Won 7 times with Barcelona.
  • Spanish Super Cup: Won 8 times with Barcelona.
  • FIFA Club World Cup: Won 3 times with Barcelona.

With Argentina National Team

  • Copa América: Won in 2021.
  • Olympic Gold Medal: Won in 2008.
  • FIFA U-20 World Cup: Won in 2005.
  • FIFA World Cup: Won in 2022 (Qatar).

Records

  • Most Goals in a La Liga Season: Scored 50 goals in the 2011-2012 season.
  • All-Time Top Scorer for Barcelona: Scored over 670 goals in official matches.
  • All-Time Top Scorer in La Liga: Scored over 470 goals in the league.

Other Titles

  • FIFA World Cup 2014 Golden Ball: Awarded despite Argentina not winning the tournament.
  • Top Scorer in South American World Cup Qualifiers: Holds the record.
